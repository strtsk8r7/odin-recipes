Description

Based on everyone's reviews below, I think it's safe to say that this is an award-winning chili recipe. It has the perfect hearty texture, the best blend of spices, and it's a cinch to make. That's why it's one of my favorite one-pot meals to make when the temperature drops. Each spoonful instantly puts a smile on my face and reminds me of just how good simple comfort foods can be.

Ingredients

  • 2 strips thick cut bacon, sliced 1/2-inch thick
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tpsp tomato paste
  • 1 (28-ounce can) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce can) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15-ounce can) dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • bay leaf
